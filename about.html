Donation

One Future Unified Team is funded via many methods and ways, with the most common from donation and afterwards, ads revenue from our ads page. We currently do not sell any and all of the basic user data we collected to maintain the website so donation is the road for revenue for the team, rather it is $1 or $5, it still matter a lot to us. Every single cent would be used in the team directly or indirectly.
